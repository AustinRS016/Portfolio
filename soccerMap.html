Birth Places of Bundesliga Soccer Players

This interactive map depicts the birth cities of Bundesliga soccer players, symbolized by team logo.

This map uses MapBox GL JS, the soccer player data is from trasnfermarkt.de, it was geolocated using the Nominatim geocoder from Geopy and overlapping coordinates were modified by me. Click here to see the python scripts used for geocoding and modifying the coordinates.
